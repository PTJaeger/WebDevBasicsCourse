About Us

Acme Rentals has been a leader in the rental industry for over 20 years. We specialize in providing top-of-the-line cranes and heavy machinery to clients across various industries. Our extensive fleet includes mobile cranes, tower cranes, and specialized lifting equipment to meet the diverse needs of our customers. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction is what sets us apart and has kept us ahead of the competition. We pride ourselves on our reliable equipment, competitive pricing, and exceptional customer service. Our teams are with the customer every step of the way.

Our Services

We provide a number of services to our clients aside from rentals. We are committed to offering free maintenance and repairs for the duration of any rental agreement. As well as that, we offer a consulting service so that you can work our experts to determine what is right for your project.
